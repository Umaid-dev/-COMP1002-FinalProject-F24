Welcome to Nike

At Nike, our mission is simple: to bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete* in the world.

Our Journey

Founded in 1964 by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight, Nike has grown from humble beginnings into one of the most recognizable brands in the world. From the first Nike waffle sole to the latest cutting-edge Flyknit and Air Max technologies, our legacy is built on a commitment to innovation, sustainability, and empowering athletes of all levels.

What We Stand For

  • Performance Innovation: Constantly exploring new ways to enhance athletic performance.
  • Sustainability: Striving for zero carbon emissions and zero waste through our Move to Zero initiative.
  • Diversity and Inclusion: Celebrating the power of sport to unify people and fostering a culture of belonging.
